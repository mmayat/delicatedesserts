Our Story


Delicate Desserts provides the very finest in delicious sweet treats, from cookie dough delicacies to delightful, fluffy waffles our experienced magicians artisans use only the finest ingredients to create enticing works of art that can be enjoyed with all the human senses.

All our desserts have been created to be irresistible, elegant and innovative, providing a special moment of pure indulgence. Our desserts are not only beautiful to look at but also offering the most luxurious taste and aroma that can only be experienced in one of our stylish outlets.

Kids are not forgotten, even though we think we’re all big kids at heart when it comes time for dessert. The whole menu is available to children plus find a few desserts specifically made for the little dessert lovers.

We never make compromises with our culinary creations, because we understand that the special moments in life should be experienced and enjoyed to the fullest.

Enjoy the special moments in life and make every day special only with Delicate Desserts.
